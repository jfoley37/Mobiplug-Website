Our Origins

TechStars up in da hood.

Mobiplug is an Internet-of-Things company that recognizes it’s not just about connecting things, but also about creating exciting services and intelligence from a network of devices. We connect thousands of existing products that are already on the market and make them work with each other.

We’re a TechStars Boulder 2012 graduate, and well funded. We’re offering exciting challenges to build tech from the ground up with a fun team that has lots of personality. And the really good news - we’re actively looking for you to join our team in Boulder, Colorado!
